About Us

InFocus Health, LLC is a home health, public health, medical transportation and courier services. We have a team of Public Health Practitioners, Nurse Practitioners, Nurses, Social Workers, Physical Therapists, Pharmacy Technicians, Certified Nurse Assistants, Home Makers and Drivers providing compassionate, comprehensive, and competent care to our clients in their homes across Texas and beyond.

We are accredited by Community Health Accreditation Program (CHAP), certified by Medicare and Medicaid, and licensed by the State of Texas. We are certified by the United States Government as a Service Veteran Owned Small Business (SDVOSB) and Historically Underutilized Business (HUB).

Our Mission

InFocus Health, LLC was established in 2010 to be a hub for comprehensive home and public health services. For people that are home bound we have the capability to meet all their health and social needs in their homes. Our clinicians (Physicians, Nurse Practitioners and Physician Assistants) perform home visits thereby obviating the need for clinic visits. Our Nurses, Physical Therapists, Occupational Therapists and other ancillary services can be provided at home. Our homemakers can perform activities of daily living (personal hygiene, cook, clean, laundry, etc.). Our courier service can pick up and deliver pharmaceutical products and other supplies to homes. None emergent medical transportation services can be provided to and from all appointments as needed. For individuals that are debilitated and need comprehensive care in their homes, InFocus Heath, LLC is the one stop shop for all your home care needs.

Our local and international public health practitioners and partners are conducting, collaborating, researching, analyzing, documenting and working on public health challenges with the intent of finding innovative solutions.
